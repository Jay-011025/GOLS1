The Garden of Languages and services (GOLS) operates at the intersection of applied linguistics and elite professional consulting. We do not rely on surface-level translation algorithms; we analyze the underlying structural syntax of communication.

The Founder's Foundation

GOLS is spearheaded by an active doctoral scholar in linguistics and a certified sworn translator. With a specialized focus on the morphosemantics and syntax of French and Haitian Creole, the agency brings university-level research directly to commercial and academic client projects.

Methodology & Technology

We build our pedagogical frameworks using the 80/20 rule and advanced digital knowledge management systems. Furthermore, GOLS holds certifications in Advanced Generative AI for teaching and learning, allowing us to engineer highly efficient, modern language acquisition tracks that far outpace traditional tutoring.
