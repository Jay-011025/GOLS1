Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Introduction

Garden of Languages and Services, also referred to as GOLS, respects the privacy of visitors, students, clients, and partners. This policy explains in a transparent way how we collect, use, and protect the information shared through our website.

2. Information collected

We may collect the following data when you complete a registration or contact form: your full name, your email address, your phone number, the type of language service requested, and any documents or texts that you voluntarily choose to submit for the delivery of a service.

3. Use of information

The information collected is used exclusively to respond to your direct requests, provide our services, communicate follow-up information, organize your personalized training path, carry out requested translations, or improve the relevance of our academic and language support.

4. Document confidentiality

Documents and texts submitted to GOLS for translation, proofreading, or revision are handled with absolute discretion and strict professional confidentiality. We do not disclose, share, or sell any document to third parties not directly involved in technical validation or the direct execution of the requested service, unless required by law.

5. Sharing of information

GOLS does not sell personal data. Certain technical information may be transmitted securely only to the third-party services required for the proper operation of our communication tools, including the Web3Forms form-processing API.

6. Data retention

Personal and textual information is kept only for the period strictly necessary to complete the requested services, support learners in their educational process, or comply with administrative and legal obligations.

7. Your rights

In accordance with applicable rules, you have the right to access, correct, update, or request deletion of your personal information. You may exercise this right at any time by contacting our team directly.

8. Contact

For any question regarding this policy or for any request to modify your data, please write to us directly at our official address:

gardenoflanguagesandservices@gmail.com
